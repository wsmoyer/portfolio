I love anything to do with being creative and I am also very interested in how things work. I am somebody who likes to learn things and I often find myself teaching myself a wide variety of things. I don’t like to leave things unfinished and sometimes I get stressed out when I can’t finish something right away. Some of the things that I value the most are hard work and honesty. If I am going to be honest the things that I am not the best at are communication. I can get my ideas across to other people and work well with others but sometimes I know I could have expressed myself better to other people. One of the people I most admire is my sister. She is a person who always does the right thing and is always honest. She is probably the person I most try to model my behavior after. I suppose the thing that makes me the most angry is when people lie. I don’t think this is something that is ever necessary and I try to avoid people who are liars. I like to watch shows to relax like most people, but honestly sometimes doing work does relax me because I know I will have the weight of that work lifted off me. I would say I am a pretty relaxed person and overall I work well under pressure.
